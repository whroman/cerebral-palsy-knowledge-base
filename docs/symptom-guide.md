## When to Seek Immediate Care

Contact a healthcare provider urgently for:
- Sudden change in function or mobility
- New or worsening seizures
- Signs of aspiration (choking, pneumonia)
- Sudden severe pain
- Signs of depression crisis (thoughts of self-harm)
- Baclofen pump malfunction symptoms (if applicable)
