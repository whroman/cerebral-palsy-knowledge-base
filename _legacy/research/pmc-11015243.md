# Association of Cerebral Palsy with Autism Spectrum Disorder and Intellectual Disability

[PMC](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC11015243/)

## Key Findings

### Autism Prevalence in Cerebral Palsy

> "6-9% of people with cerebral palsy also have autism spectrum disorder"

### Increased Odds

> "5-fold increased odds of autism in individuals with cerebral palsy compared to the general population"

### Clinical Implications

- Routine autism screening should be considered for individuals with cerebral palsy
- Co-occurrence affects treatment planning and service needs
- Understanding overlap helps prevent misdiagnosis
