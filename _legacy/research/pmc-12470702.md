# The Role of Cognitive Functioning in the ICF Framework: A Systematic Review

[PMC](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC12470702/)

## Key Findings

### Cognitive Variability

> "There is large variability in the type and severity of cognitive impairments among individuals with cerebral palsy, ranging from challenges affecting functioning in just one area to severe and global impairments. Although cognition and motor functioning are associated, there is not a one-to-one relationship."
