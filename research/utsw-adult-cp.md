# Personalized Care for Adults with Cerebral Palsy

[UT Southwestern](https://utswmed.org/medblog/adult-cerebral-palsy-care/)

## Key Findings

### Physiatrist Role

> "Physiatrists and physical medicine and rehabilitation experts guide patients in comprehensive care including functional issues, spasticity and pain management, and physical therapy exercises to retain and improve mobility, reduce fatigue, and enhance mental health in young adults"

### Leadership

> "Led by the Chair of the Department of Physical Medicine and Rehabilitation who launched a new clinic specifically for adults with cerebral palsy"
