# Gaps in transitional care to adulthood for patients with cerebral palsy: a systematic review

[PubMed](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/37552305/)

## Key Findings

> "No standardized transition tool or approach exists across programs"

> "Transition-associated poor outcomes included housing instability, unemployment, difficulty forming relationships, increased hospital admission rates, and decreased use of rehabilitation services"

### Clinical Outcomes Post-Transition
- Housing instability
- Unemployment
- Difficulty forming relationships
- Increased hospital admission rates
- Decreased use of rehabilitation services

### Systemic Barriers
- Poor communication within health service teams
- Limited adult providers accepting cerebral palsy patients
- Lack of financial resources for specialized care

### Patient Experience Gaps
- Lack of transition preparedness
- Difficulty navigating the adult system
- Gaps in seamless care
- Limited accessibility to specialists

### Protective Factors
- Family involvement
- Self-efficacy promotion
- Early introduction to adult care teams

### Study Details
- 27 observational studies analyzed
- Searched PubMed, Embase, Scopus databases
- PRISMA guidelines followed
