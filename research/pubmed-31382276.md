# Prevalence of Mental Health Disorders Among Adults With Cerebral Palsy: A Cross-sectional Analysis

[PubMed](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/31382276/)

## Key Findings

> "Adults with cerebral palsy showed significantly elevated prevalence rates compared to those without cerebral palsy"

### Prevalence in Men with Cerebral Palsy vs General Population

| Disorder Category | Adults with Cerebral Palsy | Adults without Cerebral Palsy |
|-------------------|---------------------------|------------------------------|
| Schizophrenic disorders | 2.8% | 0.7% |
| Mood disorders | 19.5% | 8.1% |
| Anxiety disorders | 19.5% | 11.1% |
| Personality/behavior disorders | 1.2% | 0.3% |
| Substance-related disorders | 4.7% | 3.0% |

- Study included 8.7 million adults (7,348 with cerebral palsy)
- Analyzed 37 mental health conditions across 6 categories
- Similar elevated patterns for women with cerebral palsy
- Those with additional neurodevelopmental conditions showed comparable or higher rates
