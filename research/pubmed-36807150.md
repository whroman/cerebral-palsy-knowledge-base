# Prevalence and incidence of chronic conditions among adults with cerebral palsy: A systematic review and meta-analysis

[PubMed](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/36807150/)

## Key Findings

> "Adults with cerebral palsy are more likely to have chronic conditions than the general population and are more likely to develop chronic conditions"

### Prevalence of Chronic Conditions

| Condition | Prevalence |
|-----------|------------|
| Scoliosis | 46% |
| Malnutrition | 38% |
| Urinary incontinence | 32% |
| Epilepsy | 28% |
| Hypertension | 26% |
| Asthma | 24% |
| Mood affective disorders | 23% |
| Depression | 21% |
| Anxiety | 21% |

### Elevated Risk vs General Population

Adults with cerebral palsy showed substantially elevated risk for:
- Type 2 diabetes
- Anxiety, bipolar disorder, depression, schizophrenia
- Hypertension, heart disease, stroke
- Asthma, osteoporosis
- Chronic kidney disease

### Study Details
- 69 studies from 18 countries
- 53 different conditions analyzed
- Both prevalence (65 studies) and incidence (13 studies) examined
