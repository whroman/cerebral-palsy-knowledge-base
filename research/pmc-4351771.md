# Prevalence of Cerebral Palsy, Co-occurring Autism Spectrum Disorders

[PMC](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC4351771/)

## Key Findings

### Co-occurring ASD Prevalence

> "The US Autism and Developmental Disabilities Monitoring Network 2008 reported that co-occurring ASD frequency was 6.9% among 8-year-old children with cerebral palsy"

### Population-based Data

This study provides population-based surveillance data on the co-occurrence of autism spectrum disorders in children with cerebral palsy.
