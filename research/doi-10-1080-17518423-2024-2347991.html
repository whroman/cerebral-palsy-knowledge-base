Cognitive Functioning and Assessment in Adults with Cerebral Palsy: A Scoping Review

Taylor & Francis

Key Findings​

Global Prevalence​

"17 million people globally have cerebral palsy"

Cognitive Assessment Challenges​

Adults with cerebral palsy face unique challenges in cognitive assessment due to:

  • Motor impairments affecting test performance
  • Communication difficulties
  • Lack of age-appropriate standardized tools

Need for Specialized Assessment​

The review highlights the need for assessment approaches that account for the motor and communication challenges common in cerebral palsy.
