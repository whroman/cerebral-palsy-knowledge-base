# Current State of Neurodevelopmental Disorders in Childhood

[BioMed Central - Child and Adolescent Psychiatry and Mental Health](https://capmh.biomedcentral.com/articles/10.1186/s13034-022-00462-1)

## Key Findings

### Comorbidity Rates

> "Among children with NDDs, 21.7% had two or more; comorbid NDDs were most common in children with ASD (79.6%), cerebral palsy (74.2%), ID (56.9%) and epilepsy (55.1%)"
