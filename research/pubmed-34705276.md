# Health service use among adults with cerebral palsy: a mixed-methods systematic review

[PubMed](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/34705276/)

## Key Findings

### Study Scope
- Mixed-methods systematic review
- Examined proportion of adults with cerebral palsy using health services
- Explored frequency of use, experiences, and perceptions

### Health Service Utilization

> "Health service use among adults with cerebral palsy, aiming to determine the proportion of adults with cerebral palsy using health services and their frequency of use, as well as exploring experiences and perceptions of health services."

### Implications
- Adults with cerebral palsy have ongoing healthcare needs throughout life
- Transition from pediatric to adult services presents challenges
- Need for better understanding of adult-specific healthcare requirements
